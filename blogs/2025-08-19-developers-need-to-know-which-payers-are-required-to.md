Our previous post, Who’s Actually Required to Provide Access to Clinical Data , covered the provider side of healthcare APIs. Now let’s complete the picture with payers: the insurance companies and government programs that pay for healthcare services. If providers hold the clinical story (diagnoses, treatments, outcomes), payers hold the financial story: claims data (billing records, coverage decisions, payment history, prior authorization).
